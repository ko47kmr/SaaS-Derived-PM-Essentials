## Audience Branching
Prompt:
Audience?
- **Exec**: High-level ROI. Focus on "Why Now" and bottom-line impact.
- **Board**: Strategic market. Focus on long-term vision and competitive landscape.
- **Investor**: Financial model. Focus on growth metrics (ARR, CAC, LTV) and exit strategy.
- **Internal**: Tech detail. Focus on feasibility, debt reduction, and team capacity.

BtoB/BtoC?
- **BtoB**: ACV, Pipeline Velocity, Retention.
- **BtoC**: Viral Coefficient, DAU/MAU, Retention.

## Web Search for Pitch Templates/Cases

- Found: "Problem-Solution-Market-Traction-Ask-Team" structure.

## Framework with Financial Model
1.  **Summary/Ask**: Clear "$X for Y".
2.  **Problem/Market**: Data urgency/Market Gap.
3.  **Solution**: Proposal/demo link.
4.  **Business Case**: ROI model (ARR/CAC/LTV).
5.  **Risks/Mitigate**: Honest assessment.
6.  **Team/Timeline/CTA**: Who and When.

## Slide Output w/Embed Demo

**Slide 5: Business Case**
ROI table: Invest $2M → ARR $10M (5x)

**Slide 6: Risks**
List + Mitigate

**Slide 7: Ask**
"$2M budget, 6 eng Q3 start"

**Delivery Notes:** 10min, Q&A ROI focus.

## Best Practices
-   **Audience tailor**: Adjust language for CFO (financial focus) vs CTO (tech focus).
-   **Numbers bold**: Highlight ROI and TAM.
-   **Demo embed**: Show, don't just tell.
-   **Under-promise**: Manage expectations effectively.
-   **Rehearse record**: Practice timing and delivery.

## Style
"Confident strategist. Numbers/story balance. Ask clear. Demo integrate."
